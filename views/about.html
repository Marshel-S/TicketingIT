TicketingIT software allows organizations to resolve their internal IT issues by streamlining the resolution process. The elements they handle, called tickets, provide context about the issues, including details, categories, and any relevant tags.



What is a Ticket?

Within an employee IT ticketing system, a ticket is a special document or record that represents an incident, alert, request, or event that requires action from the IT department. It often contains additional contextual details and may also include relevant contact information of the individual who created the ticket.


Tickets are usually employee-generated, but automated tickets may also be created when specific incidents occur and are flagged. Once a ticket is created, it is assigned to an IT agent to be resolved. Effective ticketing systems allow tickets to be submitted via a variety of methods such as virtual agents, phone, email, service portals, live agents, and walk-up experience.



How technical do organize our Ticket?

When you submit a ticket on our IT support website, your request is carefully organized so our team can handle it efficiently. Each ticket is given a unique number and stored in our system along with important details such as your issue description, the category of the problem, and the priority level. This helps our support team clearly understand your request and ensures that no ticket gets lost.

Our system also groups tickets based on the type of problem, such as software, network, or hardware issues. By organizing tickets this way, we can quickly direct your request to the right support specialist. This makes the process smoother and helps us respond to your issue as quickly and accurately as possible.



How do my Ticket has flow?

After you submit a ticket, it goes through a simple support process so our team can resolve your issue. First, your ticket is received and recorded in our system. Then it is reviewed and assigned to the appropriate support team member who has the expertise to handle your request.

Once assigned, the technician will begin working on the issue and may update the ticket if more information is needed. When the problem has been resolved, the ticket will be marked as completed. This step-by-step process helps ensure that every request is handled properly from start to finish.



How could i know where's my Ticket has brought?

You can easily track the progress of your ticket in Ticket List menu. Each ticket has its own tracking number, which allows you to check its current status at any time. This lets you see whether your request has been received, is being reviewed, or is currently being worked on by our support team.

In addition, you may receive updates whenever there is progress on your ticket. These updates help keep you informed about what is happening and which stage your request is currently in. Our goal is to make the support process transparent so you always know the status of your request.
